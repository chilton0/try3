Education

Here's what I've learned

Since stepping into my first ECON 110 lecture, I knew my life was changed. Growing up, I never thought I would study a subject so money-focused, but now I know economics is more than just money. It's people.

Growing up in Las Vegas, the education I was given before attending college largly constisted of learning to develop meaningful connections with people who had very different upbringings from my own. I love the opportunity to understand how backgrounds influence choices, and how those choices influence lives.

As I've gained a knowledge economics, I've learned that with this knowledge comes the power to make change. I look forward to pressing forward in finding real solutions to economic issues, continuing to learn all the while.
